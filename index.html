Overview

Retro90s.css is a CSS framework inspired by the golden age of the web. It brings back blinking text, 3D buttons, and nostalgia from the days of dial-up internet.

Examples

Try combining classes like .retro-button, .retro-box, and .retro-blink for authentic 90s chaos.
